<section id="mission">
  <h2>Our Mission</h2>
  <p>At HumanFirst, we believe Artificial Intelligence must evolve ethically, responsibly, and always serve humanity's best interests. Our mission is to fund education, advocacy, and development programs that promote human-centered AI through a decentralized, transparent trust.</p>
  
  <p><strong>Vision:</strong> A future where AI empowers humans, protects rights, and uplifts society — not replaces or controls it.</p>
</section>

<section id="fundraising">
  <h2>Fundraising Idea: The HumanFirst Token (HFT)</h2>
  <p>To support this mission, we are introducing the <strong>HumanFirst Token (HFT)</strong> on the Solana blockchain.</p>
  
  <ul>
    <li><strong>Symbolic Ownership:</strong> Each HFT token represents your commitment to responsible AI.</li>
    <li><strong>How it Works:</strong> Donate to the HumanFirst Trust and receive HFT tokens in return. 1 SOL donation = 1,000 HFT (example rate).</li>
    <li><strong>Use of Funds:</strong> 100% of proceeds go to the Trust to fund global education, awareness, and advocacy for AI ethics.</li>
    <li><strong>Transparency:</strong> All donations and fund uses are publicly tracked on-chain.</li>
  </ul>

  <p><strong>Important:</strong> HFT is not an investment and does not promise financial returns. It is a badge of honor for supporting a vital cause.</p>
</section>

<section id="technical">
  <h2>Technical Details (for the Curious)</h2>
  <h3>Token Basics</h3>
  <ul>
    <li><strong>Blockchain:</strong> Solana</li>
    <li><strong>Token Type:</strong> SPL Token</li>
    <li><strong>Supply:</strong> 10,000,000 HFT fixed</li>
    <li><strong>Minting:</strong> Only through verified donations</li>
    <li><strong>Trading:</strong> HFT will initially be non-tradable. Secondary market listing may be enabled in the future after community consultation.</li>
  </ul>

  <h3>Trust and Sustainability</h3>
  <p>Funds raised will not be spent freely. Instead, we will:</p>
  <ul>
    <li>Create an <strong>Endowment Fund</strong> to preserve the $10M principal raised.</li>
    <li><strong>Stake assets</strong> on the Solana network to earn sustainable passive rewards (~5-7% annually).</li>
    <li>Use only the staking rewards and new revenue streams (like memberships, certifications, and partnerships) to fund programs indefinitely.</li>
  </ul>

  <h3>Why Solana?</h3>
  <ul>
    <li>Fast transactions</li>
    <li>Low fees</li>
    <li>Environmentally friendly</li>
    <li>Large community and reliable infrastructure</li>
  </ul>
</section>
